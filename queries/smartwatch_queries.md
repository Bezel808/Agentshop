# Smartwatch Query Library

## Utilitarian Queries

**U1**

> I want a smartwatch mainly for health tracking and daily productivity. It should have accurate heart rate monitoring, sleep tracking, and long battery life.

**U2**

> I'm looking for a reliable smartwatch for fitness tracking, especially running and workouts. Accuracy and battery life are more important than design.

**U3**

> I need a smartwatch that works well with notifications, calendar, and health monitoring. Practical features matter more than appearance.

**U4**

> I want a smartwatch primarily to monitor my health and activity levels. It should be accurate and durable.

**U5**

> Recommend a smartwatch with strong fitness and health features and good battery life. Design is not my main concern.
